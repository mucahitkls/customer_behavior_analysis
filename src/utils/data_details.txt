Columns:
CustomerID (Unique identifier for the customer)
Name (Customer's full name)
Age (Customer's age)
Email (Customer's email address)
SignUpDate (Date the customer signed up, format: YYYY-MM-DD)
LastPurchaseDate (Date of the last purchase, format: YYYY-MM-DD)
TotalPurchases (Total number of purchases made by the customer)
AveragePurchaseValue (Average value of purchases)
PreferredDevice (Device most often used for shopping, e.g., Mobile, Desktop)
Location (Customer's location)
